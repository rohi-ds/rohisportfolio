Cubester

Overview

Cubester is a mobile game I created when I was younger, a game where you would alter time to help a cube maneuver through small gaps in a variety of different levels and even different game-modes. I planned to release it to the Google Playstore and use ads and in-game purchases to monetise the game.

My Role

I was the sole developer of Cubester and handled it's programming in C# with Unity to the creation of the assets it used in the game.
