Local Chatter

Safety for your town, safety for yourself.

Overview

LocalChatter is an app created as a solution to the issues of safety in your local area and accessibility to the council. Our aim is to find a way to access the council much more effectively and quicker and for them to be able to interact with ourselves, the local people.

Another of ours is to find a way to solve safety issues/hazards in the area. E.g.: A broken traffic light. This links in with contacting the council as these are all local issues that are unfortunately not solved as quickly as they should.

My Role

To develop LocalChatter, I:

  • Programmed in Java using Android Studio
  • Built the backend with Google Firebase
  • Led a team of three members
  • Created promotional materials
  • Conducted insightful surveys
  • Collaborated with politician Emer Tóibín
